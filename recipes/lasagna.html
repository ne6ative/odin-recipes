Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly.
  2. Cook the onion: Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent.
  3. Add seasoning and tomatoes Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  4. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Cook the noodles: Boil the noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  6. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  7. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
